Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our services

What is the Complete Health Check package?

The Complete Health Check package is our most comprehensive preventive health assessment that covers many parameters across all major body systems. It includes blood, liver and kidney function tests, lipid profile, thyroid profile, diabetes screening, vitamin levels, and more. This package provides a complete picture of your health status and is recommended annually for adults.

How should I prepare for my blood tests?

For most blood tests, especially those requiring fasting (like glucose and lipid profile), you should fast for 8-12 hours before the test. You can drink water during this period. Avoid alcohol and strenuous exercise for 24 hours before the test. If you're taking medications, consult your doctor about whether to take them before the test. For specific test preparations, please contact our customer care.

How long does it take to get test results?

Most routine test results are available within 24 hours. For specialized tests, it may take 48-72 hours. You will receive an SMS notification when your reports are ready. You can access your reports online through our patient portal, download them from our website, or collect physical copies from our center. For critical results, our team will contact you directly.

Do you offer home sample collection?

Yes, we provide free home collection services across Panipat and nearby areas. Our trained phlebotomists follow strict safety protocols and use sterile equipment. To schedule a home collection, you can call us at +91-9306038690, book online through our website, or message us on WhatsApp. Home collection is available from 6:00 AM to 8:00 PM, Monday to Saturday.

Am I eligible for tax exemption on preventive health packages?

Yes, under Section 80D of the Income Tax Act, expenses incurred on preventive health check-ups for self, spouse, dependent children, or parents are eligible for tax deduction up to ₹5,000 per financial year. We provide the necessary receipts and documentation for tax exemption purposes with all our health packages.
