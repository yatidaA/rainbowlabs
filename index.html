About Rainbow Laboratories

Your Trusted Diagnostic Partner

At Rainbow Laboratories, we believe that regular health check-ups are essential for early detection and prevention of diseases. Early diagnosis and timely treatment provide the best opportunity for effective management of health conditions without severe complications.

Our state-of-the-art facility in Panipat is equipped with the latest diagnostic technology and staffed by experienced pathologists and technicians. We are committed to providing accurate, reliable, and timely test results with a patient-centric approach.

Our comprehensive range of health packages are designed to cater to various needs, from basic screenings to advanced diagnostic panels. These packages can be performed as part of regular preventive health checkups every 6 to 12 months or as recommended by your healthcare provider.
